About us

Les Vins Café French restaurant in kandy has been repeatedly reviewed as one of the best dining experiences in the srilanka by food journalists and critics. If you are a fan of classic, elegant and romantic French fine dining, then book to reserve a table at Les Vins Café. Enjoy fabulous French food with a superb lunch or an intimate dinner service accompanied by a wonderful international wine list

Our Values

Les Vins Café is passionate about ensuring our guests get the very best authentic French cuisine with fantastic, affordable quality. Attentive, yet relaxed service in a beautiful environment ensure we continue to deliver a seasonal daily changing menu, with sumptuous wines and cocktails all delivered with a smile, warmth and a ‘joie de vivre’

Vission

French Bakery aims to offer an exceptional quality of baked products, fresh daily! And deliver a unique experience to the customers through providing great food, friendly & attentive service, reasonable prices in addition to a warm, welcoming environment

Mission

We are committed to total customer satisfaction. We believe in building long-term, trusting relationships with customers and team members. We also seek to increase market share in this region and continue to expand in line with the growth of Srilanka & other countries, and maintain high standards of culinary products.
